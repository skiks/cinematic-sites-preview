What makes it cinematic

  • Temple silhouette against molten-orange sunset — Bali's single most photographed view
  • Waves crashing against the rocky outcrop; at high tide the temple appears to float
  • Kecak fire dance at sunset — 50+ men chanting "cak-cak-cak" around firelight
  • One of 7 sacred sea temples forming a spiritual chain around Bali's coast
  • Sacred Ular Suci (temple sea snakes) in the caves beneath the rock
  • Tirta Pabersihan — holy spring where priests bless visitors at the rock's base

Suggested hero subject

  • Lead candidate: silhouetted temple on the rock, backlit by a molten sunset, waves crashing, camera push-in
  • Alternative: slow-motion firelight Kecak dance circle with embers rising
  • Alternative: cinematic close-up of canang sari offerings + frangipani + holy water, temple out of focus in background
