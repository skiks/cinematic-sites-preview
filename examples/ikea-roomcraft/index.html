How It Works

Three steps to a room you'll love.

Share your room dimensions & style preferences
Your 3D room preview, curated by a designer
One delivery. One assembly team. Done.
Step 01

Tell us your space

Share your room dimensions, photos, and style preferences. Our AI generates a 3D preview of your room in minutes — showing you exactly what it'll look like before anything ships.

Step 02

We design it

An IKEA interior designer curates furniture, textiles, lighting, and accessories — all coordinated, all within your budget. You approve the design before we order a single item.

Step 03

Everything arrives

One delivery. One assembly team. Your entire room — styled, built, and ready to live in. We even take away all the packaging.
